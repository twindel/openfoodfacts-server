# The phrase "Red beans" is mainly referred to "kidney beans", but in those countries where Adzuki (beans) are cultivated, it is referred to "adzuki beans". Adzuki beans are mainly grown in east asia (China, Japan, Taiwan and Korea).
# The Phrase "red mung beans" means, that "adzuki beans" are cognate (genetic) to the green "mung beans".
# The Phrase "soja roja" means, that "adzuki beans" are historically mismatched with soja and soja beans sprout.
